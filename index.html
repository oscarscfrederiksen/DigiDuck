What is
DigiDuck?

DigiDuck is a drop of programatically-generated ducks, created by Oscar Frederiksen and Coby Leykind, aiming to be fun, personable and individual. Through hand-drawn hats, facial expressions, and clothes, DigiDuck embraces what it means to be on the common ground but having a sense of individuality.

Roadmap

This roadmap outlines our goals and where we want to take DigiDuck. We have a lot of ideas and concepts that we are working on.

1. Launch

We're going to launch an initial 250 ducks ready for purchase on OpenSea. Around this time, we're also going to open up our mint on a private waitlist.

2. Community Giveaway

Next, we need to build our community. We're going to launch some fun and fantastic community-interactive competitions whether it be invite contests, quiz nights or anything else!

3. Game Drop

We'll be working hard on an online multiplayer game where you can use your duck to win competitions - the rarer your duck, the better abilities you'll have.

4. Collaboration

We'll partner with some other NFT projects to create some epic hybrids - imagine a duck-cyborg, or a duck-ape, there's endless possibilities!

5. Future Drops

Once we've gained enough traction in the community, we'll continue our expansion of ducks, unveiling hundreds of new items and features, ready to take DigiDuck to the moon!

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the mint date?

Firstly, we'll be listing some of our hand-picked NFTs for sale. Once we gain traction in the community, and our waitlist hits 100+ members, we'll open the mint, hopefully around the beginning of March.

What's an NFT?

NFT stands for non-fungible token, which basically means that it's a one-of-a-kind digital asset that belongs to you and you only.

Why DigiDuck?

Our beautiful illustration coupled with our far-reaching goals for DigiDuck make us the perfect choice. Not only will you have a lovalble character in your wallet, you'll be able to play games and find an awesome community!

What exactly will I get?

If you buy one of our NFTs through OpenSea, you'll find yourself with exactly one handsome, intelligent and all-round fantastic DigiDuck. If you purchase an egg, you'll be able to mint one yourself, with the chance of getting one of our rarest ducks.
